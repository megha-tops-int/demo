Donation requests

Junction Studio aims to support individuals, community organisations and sports clubs each year through a range of different ways. For us it’s about being genuine in giving back to the communities that do such a great job in supporting the St Kilda Football Club and contributing to a lasting positive social legacy. Whether your request is small or big, we welcome the chance to connect and hear about what you are doing.

Read this before you apply

Junction Studio will consider supporting requests that

  • Are aligned with one or more of our quarters – diversity, leadership, wellbeing and creativity
  • Generate positive social outcomes in one or more of our communities, from Port Melbourne to Portsea
  • Are requested by St Kilda Football Club members or long-time supporters.

If you think you meet this criteria and have a special request, please fill out this form and we will get back to you within three weeks… it’s a promise.
